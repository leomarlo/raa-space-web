\sectionrule{About the Event}

RAA Chess Week is a seven-day festival of chess, culture and community at RAA.SPACE in Rīga's creative Veldze quarter. Bridging the historic depth of chess with performance, craft and social play, it transforms RAA's 138 m² gallery into a living chess venue — welcoming players, artists, students, families and the culturally curious. The week culminates in a theatrical chess opera, making RAA Chess Week a singular event on Latvia's arts calendar.
