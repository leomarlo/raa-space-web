%% About
\sectionrule{About the Event}

RAA Chess Week is a seven-day festival of chess, culture and community at RAA.SPACE in Rīga's creative Veldze quarter. Bringing together a master simultaneous exhibition, a chess opera with international performers, chess theatre, a children's workshop, a poetry evening, daily chess café sessions and a closing Hand \& Brain team tournament, the festival makes chess a living art form — accessible to players, artists, families and the curious of all ages. RAA Chess Week will be a singular moment on Latvia's cultural calendar.
