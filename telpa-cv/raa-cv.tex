\section{About}
% ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

RAA is an independent cultural space located in Radošā rūpnīca Veldze in central Riga, Latvia.
It opened on \textbf{11 July 2025}.

The space hosts a diverse programme: contemporary art exhibitions, theatre performances, concerts,
poetry readings, workshops, festivals, and community gatherings.

The space comprises an exhibition hall (103\,m$^2$), an entrance foyer (35\,m$^2$), a theatre room
(13\,m$^2$) with a dedicated foyer, a backstage corridor, and access to a courtyard connecting
the entire Veldze quarter.

RAA (aka \textit{reductio ad absurdum}) leads claims to absurdity since 2025.
At RAA you do not have to be loud to be heard.

% ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{MAATE NAATRE}
% ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

MAATE NAATRE is a herbal bar located in the entrance foyer of RAA, part of Radošā rūpnīca Veldze
in central Riga. Opened on 14 March 2026, it is a small place to linger, meet others, and taste
a variety of nature-crafted drinks. The bar brings the Latvian forest to the city --- try
different cocktails from the forest.

% ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{Timeline}
% ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

\milestone{July 2025}{RAA opened its doors on 11 July 2025. Inaugural group exhibition
  \textit{In Your Shoes}, curated by Maija Kurševa, brought together seven artists from the
  Latvian Academy of Art. Poetry marathon \textit{Sinusoid} with Syntax Stupor.}

\milestone{August 2025}{Katoptron Teātris, RAA's resident theatre, opened under the direction
  of Helias Dzimoulakis. Arta Raituma led a two-day fire workshop followed by an exhibition of
  the resulting works. Solo exhibition \textit{Saules Gaismas Meklējumos} by Fricis Ripa.
  Musical evening with Elza \& Uģis Albiņš. Photography exhibition by Ksenija Kumačova.}

\milestone{September 2025}{Exhibition connected to the international theatre festival Homo Novus.
  Sewing Machine Techno. British poet Joëlle Taylor (T.S. Eliot Prize winner) gave a reading.
  Launch of Tvērums \#11. Evening with Erika Kolobanova \& Konstantins Belihs.}

\milestone{October 2025}{Three-day International Scent Art Festival (10--12 Oct) with
  international artists. res Tacta performance and workshop series. Brīvības Experience
  (16--30 Oct) --- urban installation about Riga's main street by Brunis Kibilds.}

\milestone{November 2025}{Kefirs Studio group exhibition. First Riga Upcycling Festival
  (15 Nov). Photography exhibition by Veronika Geida. \textit{Through the Looking Glass}
  --- photography by Paula Betija Antenišķe documenting Ivo Krieviņš and the Riga ballroom scene.}

\milestone{December 2025}{WunderKombināts publication launch. Piano recital with
  Andrejs Osokins. Tvērums December issue launch.}

\milestone{January 2026}{Winter break and renovation works at RAA.}

\milestone{February 2026}{30-day evolving installation \textit{AS DISTANT, SO NEAR} with
  various artists, exploring themes of distance, proximity, and presence.}

\milestone{March 2026}{Opening of MAATE NAATRE forest bar (14 Mar). Three research meetup
  sessions. Book presentation. Weavers group exhibition and performance
  \textit{Dialogo ad Infinitum} by Leonhard Horstmeyer.}

\milestone{April 2026}{\textit{ALTERNATĪVA: Traces from the Underground} --- exhibition by
  the 2ANNAS film festival (10 Apr -- 4 May), with four artists and three guided tours,
  supported by Rīgas dome and VKKF.}
