BIO Vanessa Vanessa Brzić

Vanessa Brzić works across theoretical physics, experimental theatre, photography, and perfor-
mance. Originally from Zagreb, she has lived and worked in Vienna and Japan, and is currently
based in Paris, where she is pursuing a PhD in Foundations of Quantum Physics and Quantum
Information Theory at Sorbonne Universit´e.
For ten years, she was part of the ZKM ensemble in Zagreb, where she worked in experimental
theatre and explored ways of deconstructing conventional performance forms. Since moving to
Paris, she has begun exhibiting her photography, and in 2024 participated in a photo exhibition
at Cit´e Universitaire. The promotional image for this performance originates from a cyanotype
preparation she created for that exhibition.
In 2024, through an art-science residency, she began working with conceptual artist Ben Bogart.
What started as a scientific contribution to the residency developed into an ongoing creative collab-
oration exploring process philosophy, relationalism, and the interface between quantum theory and
artistic practice. Brzić is now increasingly drawn toward conceptual and performance art, inspired
by Fluxus and process philosophy, and concerned with the nuances of human experience amid rapid
technological and scientific change.
