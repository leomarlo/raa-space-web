
“Presence in Absence” is a series by artist Lisette Ros that she started in Mexico-City in 2023, deriving from her research "The Absence of Metamorphosis" (2023). In this series she focuses on the perceptible act of presence in absence by examining ‘The Absent Body’. In the live performance “Presence in Absence” Piece three, Ros grapples with her own presence, manipulating control and consciousness by taking-in a natural hypnotic. This has been part of her nightly ritual since 2014, as she suffers from DSPS (Delayed Sleep Phase Syndrome).
