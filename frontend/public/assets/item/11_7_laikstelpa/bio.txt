Ilze Mazpane

Ilze Mazpane is a performance artist from Rīga with a background in philosophy, visual art, experimental theatre and creative writing. She creates site-specific durational works marked by poetic sensibility and a sensitive relationship with space.

Jana Zariņa

Jana Zariņa is primarily a historical keyboard and sound artist whose work spans early music, contemporary improvisation, electro-acoustic performance and interdisciplinary arts.
