      \sectionrule{About the Event}

      \small
      ITEM\,—\,Shuffled Time is a seven-day performative week at RAA.SPACE in R\={i}ga.
      Each day one artist or artist group occupies RAA's main hall — a 138\,m\textsuperscript{2}
      space with ten windows facing Mat\={i}sa iela — for a 24-hour period to \textbf{I}nvoke,
      \textbf{T}emper, \textbf{E}late, \textbf{M}int the space. Performances may span the full
      24 hours or take place over a shorter duration with one or more openings. The programme
      brings together seven distinct artistic voices across durational performance, body art,
      experimental poetry, aromagraphy and participatory installation.
      Curated by RAA and supported by Veldze Rado\v{s}\={a} R\={u}pn\={i}ca.

%% ── 1 · Cocoons ─────────────────────────────────────────
\perfcard%
  {Sunday, 5 July}%
  {Cocoons}%
  {Maruta Gredzena}%
  {img/item-cocoons.png}%
  {Visitors are invited to choose and wear a handmade black or white paper cocoon
   based solely on their mood — a silent, non-verbal act of self-disclosure.
   Their accumulated choices collectively reshape the atmosphere of the space,
   making each visitor both audience and co-author of the work. A paper sculpture
   installation, with objects and strips descending from the ceiling, provides the
   spatial and conceptual backdrop.}%
  {Maruta Gredzena is a sensory-driven figurative sculptor currently moving towards
   abstraction. She has mastered the art of paper objects and installations.
   IG: @em.gaa}%

%% ── 2 · Laiks Telpā ─────────────────────────────────────
\perfcard%
  {Monday, 6 July}%
  {Laiks Telp\={a}}%
  {Ilze Mazpane \& Jana Zari\c{n}a}%
  {img/item-laikstelpa.jpg}%
  {A durational performance that, using only objects found on site within RAA's
   existing space, creates a musical-bodily-spatial experience of subjective time.
   The work explores how time expands, contracts, rushes, stops and disappears —
   without clock-time as guide or constraint.}%
  {Ilze Mazpane is a performance artist from R\={i}ga with a background in
   philosophy, visual art, experimental theatre and creative writing. She creates
   site-specific durational performances marked by poetic sensibility and a
   sensitive relationship with space — works that resist the tyranny of clock-time.
   Jana Zari\c{n}a — details to follow.}%

%% ── 3 · N.3 ──────────────────────────────────────────────
\perfcard%
  {Tuesday, 7 July}%
  {N.3}%
  {Ernests \={A}bi\c{k}is}%
  {none}%
  {Ernests arrives with a rope, wraps it around one of the gallery's columns, ties
   the end to his leg and walks outward in widening circles — both within and beyond
   the gallery walls — seeking distance within constraint, until physical exhaustion
   or external factors intervene. He remains silent throughout. Intended duration:
   18:00–24:00. Visual impression to follow.}%
  {In his practice, Ernests \={A}bi\c{k}is devotes himself to playful exploration
   of the mundane and undefinable aspects of reality within unrestricted forms of
   expression.}%

%% ── 5 · Presence in Absence, Piece Two ───────────────────
\perfcard%
  {Thursday, 9 July}%
  {Presence in Absence, Piece Two}%
  {Lisette Ros}%
  {img/item-presence.jpeg}%
  {Ros will cover the gallery's expansive windows entirely with a custom-developed
   white mixture including kefir, obscuring the space from within and concealing
   any activity from the street. Once the mixture has hardened, she will slowly
   scrape it away with bare nails — revealing the space incrementally, and blurring
   the boundary between intervention, everyday action and performance. The scraped
   material will accumulate in the space as an evolving installation.}%
  {Lisette Ros (b.\,1991, NL) is a Dutch conceptual and performance artist with
   Indonesian roots. Identifying as queer, woman and humanimal, her work investigates
   identity as a construct formed through social systems and embodied experience.
   Presented internationally at the Venice Architecture Biennale, SXSW, Van
   Abbemuseum, Goethe Institute, MWoods Beijing, Casa L\"{u} CDMX. 2021 residency
   with Marina Abramović.}%

%% ── 6 · Gluttony ─────────────────────────────────────────
\perfcard%
  {Friday, 10 July}%
  {Gluttony}%
  {Kristi\={a}na \v{S}uksta \& Sandris Murins}%
  {img/item-gluttony.jpg}%
  {An experimental aromagraphy performance pairing twelve words — each coupled with
   a distinct aroma — performed in continuously reshuffled sequences and tempos.
   Through congruent and incongruent combinations of language and scent,
   \textit{Gluttony} reveals how olfactory experience can reinforce, contradict or
   transform the meaning of text — exploring desire, memory and the sensory
   dimensions of meat consumption. Meaning emerges through ever-changing temporal
   configurations rather than a fixed sequence.}%
  {Sandris Murins is a Latvian artist and inventor of the Scent Camera, founder
   of the Scent + Art Festival, promoting aromagraphy as an emerging artistic
   language. Kristi\={a}na \v{S}uksta is a Latvian poet, performance artist and
   technical producer of the festival Starptelpa. Her practice combines poetry and
   live performance to explore personal experience and human relationships through
   wit and humor.}%

%% ── 7 · Time Scores ──────────────────────────────────────
\perfcard%
  {Saturday, 11 July}%
  {Time Scores}%
  {Vanessa Brzi\v{c}}%
  {img/item-timescores.jpg}%
  {Audience members draw Fluxus-inspired event-score cards and perform short
   instructions involving body, objects, sound and space — rope, mirror, water,
   scissors, an apple. Each action inherits the accumulated state left by all
   previous ones. As the room fills with traces, the same score returns but
   under entirely changed conditions — making the room a living metaphor for
   social and historical time: every act opens new possibilities while carrying
   the weight of what came before.}%
  {Vanessa Brzi\v{c} is a cross-disciplinary artist and OIST QISS Fellow working
   at the intersection of fundamental research, quantum information and artistic
   practice. Details being confirmed.}%
